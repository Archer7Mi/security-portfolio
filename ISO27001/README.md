# ISO 27001

This folder is for ISO 27001 (GRC) artefacts I can share publicly.

I’m not trying to “fake an audit” — the goal is to show that I understand how policies, risks, and controls connect to real operations.

## What I’m putting here
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) template + examples
- risk register templates (and a portfolio-safe sample)
- short notes: how a control reduces a specific risk

## What it demonstrates
- basic ISMS thinking (scope, owners, review cycles)
- writing clear docs that could be used in a real org
- practical risk analysis (not just theory)

## How it ties to SOC / governance / risk
- SOC: policies and controls define how incidents are handled
- Governance: documentation and accountability (owners, reviews)
- Risk: choosing controls based on real risks and impact
